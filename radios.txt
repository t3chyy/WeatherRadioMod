Rarity system
-------------
Common (craftable, can be found in villages & dungeons, high odds)
Uncommon (craftable)
Rare (uncraftable, only obtainable by cleric villager trades or world exploration i.e pyramids, mineshafts, shipwrecks, medium odds)
Mythical (uncraftable, only obtainable by ancient cities or end city, 1% odds)
